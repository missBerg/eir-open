Open-source applications for accessing, viewing, and understanding Swedish medical records from 1177.se with complete privacy — your data never leaves your device.

**Perfect for:** Patients, healthcare developers, privacy-conscious medical record management.

<Aside type="note" title="Privacy">
  All processing happens locally — your data never leaves your device. API keys are stored in Keychain.
</Aside>

## Desktop App — Eir Viewer (macOS)

A native macOS app (Swift/SwiftUI) for viewing `.eir` medical record files with AI-powered health insights.

<div class="features-grid not-content mt-4">
  <FeatureCard title="Timeline view" icon="calendar">
    Entries grouped by month with colored category badges and AI-powered health insights.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="AI chat" icon="message-circle">
    Ask questions about your records using OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, or any OpenAI-compatible provider.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Agent with tools" icon="wrench">
    The AI can search records, drill into entries, find clinics, and remember you across sessions.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Smart context" icon="brain">
    Handles 400+ entries by automatically summarizing records and letting the AI fetch details on demand.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Multi-profile support" icon="users">
    Manage records for multiple family members, each with their own chat history.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Health data browser" icon="globe">
    Embedded 1177.se browser with API-based extraction and session keep-alive.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Local vector search" icon="search">
    On-device embeddings for semantic search across all your records.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Find Care" icon="map-pin">
    Interactive map with 17,800+ Swedish healthcare clinics.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Explain with AI" icon="lightbulb">
    One-click button on each journal entry to get a plain-language explanation.
  </FeatureCard>
</div>

**[Download latest release](https://github.com/BirgerMoell/eir-open-apps/releases/latest)**

## iOS App — Eir Viewer (iPhone & iPad)

A native iOS app (Swift/SwiftUI) for viewing `.eir` medical record files on iPhone and iPad with AI chat and Apple HealthKit integration.

<div class="features-grid not-content mt-4">
  <FeatureCard title="Journal viewer" icon="clipboard-list">
    Timeline of medical records with search, filtering, and category badges.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="AI chat" icon="message-circle">
    Ask questions about your records using OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, or custom providers.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Agent with tools" icon="wrench">
    Search records, get details, summarize health data, and persistent memory across sessions.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Apple HealthKit" icon="heart">
    Import vitals, activity, and lab values — heart rate, blood pressure, SpO2, weight, steps, workouts, and more.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Multi-profile" icon="users">
    Manage records for multiple family members on a single device.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Mac transfer" icon="smartphone">
    Scan QR code to receive records from the macOS app via local network.
  </FeatureCard>
</div>

Build from source with Xcode 16+ (requires iOS 17+):

```bash
cd iOS/EirViewer
xcodegen generate
open EirViewer.xcodeproj
```

## Chrome Extension — 1177 Journal Downloader

A Chrome extension that downloads your medical records from 1177.se into portable, structured files.

<div class="features-grid not-content mt-4">
  <FeatureCard title="One-click download" icon="download">
    Floating button on 1177.se exports all your journal entries.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Structured EIR format" icon="file-text">
    Exports to both plain text (.txt) and the open EIR format (.eir/YAML).
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Complete history" icon="book-open">
    Automatically loads all available entries by expanding the full timeline.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Privacy-first" icon="lock">
    All processing happens locally in your browser.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard title="Transfer to Eir.Space" icon="rocket">
    Optionally view your records in the <a href="https://eir.space">Eir.Space</a> web viewer.
  </FeatureCard>
</div>

### Installation

1. Download the [Chrome Extension](https://github.com/BirgerMoell/eir-chrome-plugin/releases/latest)
2. Load unpacked in Chrome at `chrome://extensions/`
3. Navigate to [journalen.1177.se](https://journalen.1177.se) and use the download button
