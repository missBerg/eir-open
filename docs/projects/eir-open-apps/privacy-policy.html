Privacy Policy for 1177 Journal Downloader

Last updated: January 2025

Important: This extension processes sensitive medical data. All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

1. Data Collection and Use

This Chrome extension:

  • Accesses your 1177.se journal data only when you explicitly click the download button
  • Processes medical information including diagnoses, treatments, medications, and other health records
  • Creates downloadable files containing your journal data in text format
  • Does NOT collect, store, or transmit any data to external servers

2. Data Storage

All data processing occurs locally on your device:

  • Journal data is temporarily processed in your browser's memory
  • Downloaded files are saved to your local device only
  • No data is stored in the extension or transmitted over the internet
  • No cookies or tracking mechanisms are used

3. Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To access the current 1177.se journal page
  • storage: To remember user preferences (if any)
  • tabs: To open the 1177.se journal page from the popup

4. Third-Party Services

This extension:

  • Does not integrate with any third-party services
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not communicate with external servers
  • Only interacts with the official 1177.se website

5. Data Security

Your medical data security is our priority:

  • All processing occurs locally in your browser
  • No data leaves your device
  • Downloaded files are stored only on your local device
  • You are responsible for securing downloaded files

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use or not use this extension at any time
  • Delete downloaded files from your device
  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

7. Compliance

This extension is designed to comply with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • 1177.se Terms of Service (as applicable)

8. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing or GitHub repository.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

This privacy policy is effective as of January 2025 and applies to all users of the 1177 Journal Downloader Chrome extension.
